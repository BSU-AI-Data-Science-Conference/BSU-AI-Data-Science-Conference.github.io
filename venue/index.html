Directions

Bowie State University is conveniently located near three major airports in the Washington, D.C., and Baltimore area. From Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI), the university is approximately 20 miles south taking around 30-40 minutes by car. From Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA), Bowie State is about 25 miles northeast and can be accessed via US-50 East or the I-495 Beltway, typically taking 40-50 minutes by car. From Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD), the university is around 50 miles east, with the fastest route being VA-267 and the I-495 Beltway, requiring about an hour to drive. Public transportation options include MARC trains and Metrobus routes connecting to nearby Bowie State MARC station for convenient access to campus.
